Management Commentary
FY2025 revenue beat budget by $354M (+3.3%), driven by take rate expansion to 13.9% and GMV recovery to $79.6B — the first dual acceleration since FY2020. Operating income missed by $323M, entirely attributable to a $467M G&A overage from one-time tax and legal settlements. FCF compressed 24% YoY, funded via net debt expansion ($3.2B→$3.8B) alongside $2.5B in buybacks.
FCF Bridge FY2024→FY2025 $mm · waterfall
FCF compressed ($472M) YoY despite Op CF improvement. The $1.4B working capital swing was structural — accounts payable timing shifted as eBay settled vendor agreements. Tax/other benefit partially offset. Meanwhile $2.5B buybacks were funded via net debt expansion ($3.2B→$3.8B). FCF quality is recovering in Q1 2026.

What is this?
AI-Powered Variance Commentary Engine
In a real FP&A role, writing variance commentary — the narrative that explains why actuals beat or missed budget — is one of the most time-consuming parts of month-end close. A finance team spends hours turning a spreadsheet of numbers into executive-ready prose. This engine automates that: paste any budget vs. actuals table, set your audience, and get CFO-ready commentary in 60 seconds.
Loads sample data · builds prompt · shows AI commentary output — all in one click
60s
Time to commentary
3
Audience modes
Input Paste variance table · pipe-delimited or tab-separated
Expected format:  Metric | Budget | Actual | Var $ | Var % | RAG
Accepts pipe ( | ) or tab-delimited. Header row optional. RAG column optional (G/R/A or GREEN/RED/AMBER).
Output · Load demo to see a live example
Hit ▶ Run Live Demo in the banner above to see the engine in action — or paste your own variance table and click Build prompt.
How it works
1
Paste variance table
Any Budget vs. Actuals table — pipe or tab-delimited. Pulled directly from Excel close package.
2
Set audience
CFO = terse, number-first. Finance Director = root cause per line. Board = no jargon, forward-looking.
3
Build & paste prompt
Engine injects RED-flagged rows as priority misses and embeds FP&A communication rules into the prompt.
4
Get commentary
Paste into Claude → structured Executive Summary, Key Variances, and Risk & Outlook in 60 seconds.
